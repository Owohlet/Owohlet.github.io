October 2010 1pm

I turned and saw my classmates at a distance, everyone minding their business, very much still thinking about the lecture we just had, turning back I met an old acquaintance, one look at my face, he decided that I needed the cold water he just bought more than he did. Three huge gulps sent millions of nerves traveling down my spine, we stayed there and discussed at length, he told me about an idea he had, already underway -he called it cgossip and he wanted me to be part of the team, that day I came to have a little idea of what it means to run a startup.
